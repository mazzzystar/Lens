# Privacy Policy for Lens

**Last updated:** December 17, 2025

## Overview

Lens is a browser extension that analyzes selected text using AI. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

Lens collects and processes the following data:

- **Selected text**: When you right-click and choose "Lens", the text you selected is sent to your chosen AI provider (OpenRouter, DeepSeek, or OpenAI) for analysis.
- **API key**: Your API key is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's sync storage.
- **Settings**: Your preferences (provider, model, language) are stored locally in your browser.

## Data Transmission

- Selected text is sent **directly** to your chosen AI provider's API (OpenRouter, DeepSeek, or OpenAI).
- **No data passes through our servers** â€” there are no intermediate servers.
- All API requests use HTTPS encryption.

## Data Storage

- All settings and API keys are stored **locally in your browser** using Chrome's storage API.
- No data is stored on external servers by Lens.

## Third-Party Services

When you use Lens, your selected text is sent to one of these third-party AI providers based on your settings:

- [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/privacy)
- [DeepSeek](https://www.deepseek.com/privacy)
- [OpenAI](https://openai.com/privacy)

Please review their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle data.

## Analytics and Tracking

Lens does **not** include any analytics, tracking, or telemetry.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally in your browser:
- You can view your settings by clicking the Lens extension icon
- You can delete all data by removing the extension from Chrome
- You can clear your API key at any time through the extension popup

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.
